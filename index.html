Arabic

Al-Imran

The course introduces learners of Arabic to the sound and writing systems of this language and provides them with basic structural and lexical knowledge to enable them to say things in Arabic, such as greeting others, thanking someone, introducing oneself, describing one’s background, seeking and providing info and so forth. The ability to perform these language functions in real-life or lifelike situations is developed by engaging the learner in structured functional activities and grammatical exercises.

  • Client: Student who have finised Hifz
  • Category: Iilm

Fikh

AL-Imran

The course examines the definitions, scope and history of Fiqh and Uṣūl al Fiqh. This includes the development of the different schools of Islamic jurisprudence, as well as the bases for its legal maxims. The course discusses the fundamental premises of the Sharῑ‘ah and its objectives. The course provides an exposure to the processes involved in deducing Islamic legal rulings, including the dynamics of Ijtihād and the dangers of Taqlῑd.

  • Client: Students who have finised Hifz
  • Category: Iilm

  • Client: Any student
  • Category: Hifz
